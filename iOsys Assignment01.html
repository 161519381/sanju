PERINATAL MOOD AND ANXIETY DISORDERS THE MAIN DIAGNOSIS

The term Perinental Mood and Anxiety Disorders is used to encomposs Depression in the pre-partum(before),antepartum(during) and postpartum (after) periods and effects approximately 1 in 7 women for each pregnancy experience. While "Postpartum Depression" is the most known, "postpartum" in fact means "after pregnancy".

DEPRESSION
One of several mental illnesses affecting 20% of mothers during and/ or after pregnancy

ANXIETY
Postpartum Anxiety affects more than 10% of pregnant and postpartum women. Racing, intrusive thoughts are common and can worsen if untreated.
